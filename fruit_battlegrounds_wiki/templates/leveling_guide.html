Leveling Guide

Leveling up in Fruit Battlegrounds is essential to unlock new maps, skills, and become stronger. This guide will help you level up efficiently and reach the maximum level faster.

Quick Tips

  • Boss hunting is the fastest way to gain EXP
  • Server hop to find Marco spawning soon (most bosses spawn every 2 hours)
  • Katakuri spawns every 1 hour and 30 minutes
  • Use the 2X EXP gamepass if you can afford it
  • Onigashima (Wano) provides a 2.5x EXP boost
  • AFK World allows for passive gem earning

Leveling Methods

Method 1: Boss Hunting

Best For: All levels, especially 40+

Boss hunting is the most efficient way to gain EXP in Fruit Battlegrounds. Each boss drops a large amount of EXP when defeated, based on the damage you deal to them.

Strategy:
  1. Server Hopping: Find a server where a boss is about to spawn. Look at the server time in the bottom right of the screen - most bosses spawn every two hours, Katakuri spawns every 1h 30m.
  2. Dealing Damage: You need to deal a minimum amount of damage to receive maximum rewards. For Marco, it's around 500 damage.
  3. Switching Fruits: After dealing enough damage, you can reset your character and switch to a lower-level fruit to receive EXP for it when the boss dies.
Boss Location Max EXP Level Requirement
Marco Dressrosa (Flower Hill) 15,000 None
Katakuri Mirror World (via Whole Cake) 18,000 100
Cake Queen Whole Cake 20,000 100
Kaido Onigashima (Wano) 24,000 200+

Method 2: Spamming Moves on Players or Anything

Best For: Low levels (1-40)

At lower levels, you can gain EXP by using your abilities on other players. This method is not as efficient as boss hunting but can be useful when bosses aren't available.

Strategy:
  • Find populated areas where many players are fighting
  • Use your abilities on players (you don't need to defeat them to gain EXP)
  • Focus on AoE (Area of Effect) abilities to hit multiple players at once
  • Avoid targeting the same player repeatedly as this reduces EXP gain

Method 3: The Tournament

Best For: Medium to high levels with good PvP skills

The Tournament mode can provide good EXP if you're skilled enough to win matches consistently. Each match win gives you 2,000 EXP, and winning the tournament awards 5,000 EXP plus 100 gems.

Tournament Requirements:
  • All abilities unlocked on your fruit
  • Good PvP skills (you'll face other players with their best fruits)
  • Access to Dressrosa's Colosseum (talk to Gatz NPC)

While this method isn't as consistent as boss hunting, it can be a good alternative when waiting for bosses to spawn.

Method 4: AFK World (for gem farming)

Best For: Passive gem earnings while not actively playing

AFK World allows you to earn gems passively without actively playing. This is a great option for when you're busy or away from your device.

How to Use AFK World:
  1. Access the AFK World option from the main menu or lobby
  2. Join and leave your character in the AFK area
  3. Your character will automatically earn gems over time
  4. You can collect your rewards when you return
Note: The AFK earning rate is significantly slower than active play methods, but requires no effort on your part. AFK World only provides gems, not EXP.

Level-Up Roadmap

Level 1-50: Easy

Focus on using abilities on players or fighting Marco. Stick to Dressrosa.

Level 50-100: Medium

Server hop for Marco. Start building a good fruit for boss fights.

Level 100-200: Challenging

Access Whole Cake (Level 100+) and defeat Cake Queen. Fight Katakuri in Mirror World.

Level 200-300: Difficult

Access Onigashima (Wano) for 2.5x EXP boost. Fight Kaido for maximum EXP.

EXP Boosters

  • 2X EXP Gamepass 2x Multiplier
  • Onigashima (Wano) 2.5x Multiplier
  • Whole Cake 1.75x Multiplier
  • Dressrosa 1.0x Multiplier
Tip: These multipliers stack! With the 2X EXP gamepass in Wano, you can get 5x normal EXP.

Leveling New Fruits Fast

To level up a new fruit quickly, follow these steps:

  1. Server hop until you find a boss spawning soon
  2. Equip your main high-level fruit
  3. Deal enough damage to the boss for max EXP (500+ for Marco)
  4. Reset your character (or wait for combat to end if you value your bounty)
  5. Equip the new fruit and stay in the fruit inventory section
  6. Wait for the boss to die and you'll get the EXP for the new fruit
Important: Don't spawn in as you may risk the boss dying while you're in a safe zone, which gives you no EXP.

Mobile Farming Tips

For mobile players looking to farm EXP efficiently, auto clickers can be a helpful tool:

  • GC Auto Clicker: A popular option for Android users that allows you to set up multiple tap points with custom timing
  • Auto Clicker by True Developers Studio: Another reliable option available on Play Store
  • Find More Options: Search "auto clicker for Roblox mobile" on YouTube or Google for detailed tutorials and setup guides
How to Set Up an Auto Clicker:
  1. Download both Auto Clicker (by True Developers Studio) and GC Auto Clicker from your app store
  2. Open both apps and follow the initial setup instructions for each
  3. Enable Multi-Target mode in Auto Clicker and Single-Target mode in GC Auto Clicker, then launch Roblox and join Fruit Battlegrounds
  4. Using Auto Clicker (Multi-Target), place tap points over your fruit's ability buttons (typically 2 taps per button with slight delay)
  5. Set the click interval for GC Auto Clicker (Single-Target) to 100 milliseconds and place its tap point usually over the main attack/confirm button (adjust as needed)
  6. Start both auto clickers and monitor briefly to ensure they are working correctly. Enjoy AFK farming!
Tip: Look for YouTube tutorials specific to Fruit Battlegrounds for optimal tap patterns and timing settings. Here's a helpful video guide for these steps: Video Guide.
Warning: Using third-party software like auto-clickers may violate Roblox's terms of service. Use at your own risk.
Rock Clipping for AFK EXP

Rock clipping is a technique some players use to gain EXP passively while AFK. It involves positioning your character in a specific spot, often near a rock or wall, where enemy NPCs cannot hit you, but your abilities can still reach and damage them. This is often combined with an auto-clicker to continuously use abilities.

Common Clipping Locations:
  • Dressrosa Cave Entrance: Certain spots near the entrance/exit of the Soru cave.
  • Whole Cake Island: Look for indented walls or areas behind large structures where NPCs gather.
  • Onigashima (Wano): Some elevated positions or corners might offer protection.
  • Note: Specific spots change frequently due to map updates. Experimentation is often required.
Finding and Using Clip Spots:
  1. Scout Potential Areas: Look for rocks, walls, or map geometry with small gaps, overhangs, or ledges where you might fit but NPCs can't pathfind to.
  2. Test the Spot: Position yourself and see if nearby NPCs can attack you. Lure them closer if needed.
  3. Choose the Right Fruit: Use fruits with large Area of Effect (AoE) abilities (like Magma's eruption, Light's beams) or abilities that can pass through terrain (like Barrier's attacks).
  4. Set Up Auto-Clicker (Optional): If using one, configure tap points for your chosen abilities with appropriate delays. Ensure the sequence targets the area where NPCs congregate. (Use at your own risk).
  5. Monitor Occasionally: Check back sometimes to ensure you haven't been killed, stuck, or reported, and that the spot still works.
Risks: Using third-party software like auto-clickers may violate Roblox's terms of service. Furthermore, abusing glitches like rock clipping could lead to warnings or bans if detected or reported. Spots can also be patched out by developers. Use these techniques cautiously and at your own risk.
